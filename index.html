Crystals Collector!

You will be given a randoom number in the beginning of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amoount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to a random bymber, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
